About Us

TIC Club is a dynamic technical club that focuses on improving and enhancing various aspects of life through the power of technology. Our mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and skill development among tech enthusiasts. Whether it's software development, hardware innovation, or digital design, we strive to use cutting-edge technology to create impactful solutions.

At TIC Club, we believe in continuous learning, teamwork, and pushing the boundaries of technological advancements to make a difference in the world. We organize workshops, hackathons, tech talks, and project collaborations that enrich the technical ecosystem of our community.

Our vision is to become a leading platform for technological innovation, connecting passionate individuals and providing them with the resources and mentorship needed to transform ideas into reality.

Club History

TIC Club has grown from a small initiative to a comprehensive technical community, making significant strides in fostering innovation and technical excellence.

2022

Club Foundation

TIC Club was founded by Pratik More and co-founded by Yash Bari with a vision to create an innovative technical community. The club was initially led by Kaustubh Nimbalkar and Nikhileshwar Jagatale.

2023

New Leadership & Expansion

Leadership transitioned to Yash Bari, marking a new chapter in the club's journey. The club expanded significantly, establishing 10 specialized departments:

  • Product Development
  • Finance and Sponsorship
  • Research and Development (R&D)
  • Technology Education
  • Innovation and Creativity
  • Event Management
  • Marketing and Public Relations
  • Technical Support and Infrastructure
  • Common Support
  • Community Engagement and Outreach
2024

Growing Impact

The club continues to expand its influence, organizing workshops, hackathons, and technical events that bring together students passionate about technology and innovation.
